Workflow

  • Tracks built using both MIDI and samples for flexible adaptation.
  • Instrumentation can be swapped to match different aesthetics.
  • Use of EQ and filters for clarity and mix cleanliness.
  • Rapid loop prototyping: 1–2 minutes per hour when focused.

Approach

I combine reference-based direction (scale, BPM, instrumentation) with controlled freestyle exploration to shape emotional tone while remaining production-ready for games.
