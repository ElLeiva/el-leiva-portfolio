About

I make games that evoke emotion through simple mechanics and a strong, cohesive atmosphere.

I come from a self-directed background, learning game development alongside art, music, and programming, and gradually shaping my work through small, finished projects.

My workflow is built around rapid prototyping, testing ideas early, and polishing only once a concept proves itself. I value iteration over perfection and execution over theory.

This approach allows me to focus on clarity, mood, and player experience, ensuring that every system supports the intended feeling of the game.
